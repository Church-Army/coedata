# Package index

## All functions

- [`coe_census()`](https://church-army.github.io/coedata/reference/coe_census.md)
  : Get census data for the Church of England at a parish, diocesan or
  national level.

- [`coe_census_datasets()`](https://church-army.github.io/coedata/reference/coe_census_datasets.md)
  : Browse all available datasets of ONS census 2021 data

- [`coe_census_diocese()`](https://church-army.github.io/coedata/reference/coe_census_diocese.md)
  : Obtain diocese-level census statistics on a given topic

- [`coe_census_england()`](https://church-army.github.io/coedata/reference/coe_census_england.md)
  : Obtain national level census statistics on a given topic

- [`coe_census_parish()`](https://church-army.github.io/coedata/reference/coe_census_parish.md)
  : Obtain parish-level census statistics on a given topic

- [`coe_churches()`](https://church-army.github.io/coedata/reference/coe_churches.md)
  : Get data on the Anglican geography on Church of England Churches,
  including their parish, benefice, arcdeaconry and diocese.

- [`coe_esri_fields()`](https://church-army.github.io/coedata/reference/coe_esri_fields.md)
  :

  View fields that can be passed to the `where` argument of
  [`coe_churches()`](https://church-army.github.io/coedata/reference/coe_churches.md)

- [`coe_parish_deprivation()`](https://church-army.github.io/coedata/reference/coe_parish_deprivation.md)
  [`coe_parish_imd()`](https://church-army.github.io/coedata/reference/coe_parish_deprivation.md)
  : Get parish-level deprivation data from the Church of England

- [`coe_parish_from_church()`](https://church-army.github.io/coedata/reference/coe_parish_from_church.md)
  : Get the parish codes of any number of Church of England churches.

- [`coe_parish_snapshot()`](https://church-army.github.io/coedata/reference/coe_parish_snapshot.md)
  : Get a list of comparative table(s) of local, diocesan and national
  data for a given parish

- [`ns_sec_descriptions`](https://church-army.github.io/coedata/reference/ns_sec_descriptions.md)
  : National statistics socio-economic classification levels
